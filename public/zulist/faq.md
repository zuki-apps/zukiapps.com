# FAQ — ZuList - Manage & Share Lists

Canonical page: https://zukiapps.com/zulist

## What is ZuList?

A collaborative shopping list app. Create lists, share with others, and sync in real time on iOS and Android.

## Is ZuList free?

Yes. Free tier includes up to 5 lists, 150 items per list, and sharing with one other person per list. Premium removes limits and ads.

## Do I need an account?

You can start as a guest. Sign in to sync across devices and accept invites reliably.

## Which languages are supported?

English, Hebrew, Arabic, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese (Simplified), Japanese, and Korean.

## How fast is sync?

Usually instant when online. Offline changes upload when connectivity returns.

## Can two people edit at the same time?

Yes. Last write wins for the same field; the list stays consistent.

## How do I remove someone from a list?

List owner → Members → remove. They lose access immediately.

## Why doesn’t the widget match the list I’m viewing?

The widget always shows your **primary list** from Settings.

## How do I add the widget?

See [How to: Home screen widget](./how-to-home-widget.md).

## What does Premium include?

Unlimited lists, items, and members; no ads; advanced statistics; themes; premium offline features; cloud backup.

## How do I cancel?

Manage subscription in the App Store or Google Play—ZuList updates automatically via store webhooks.

## Where is my data stored?

Firebase (Google Cloud). Lists are private to you and invited members.
