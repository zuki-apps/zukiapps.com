## What is Football Trivia Master?

Free football quiz with **450+ timed questions**, four themed categories, streak scoring, and global leaderboards. Independent entertainment by Zuki Apps — not affiliated with FIFA or any league.

## Getting started

**Q: Is it free?**  
A: Yes. Optional ads between sessions; one-time purchase removes ads in Settings.

**Q: Account required?**  
A: No account to play. Sign in with Game Center (iOS) or Play Games (Android) for leaderboards only.

**Q: Languages?**  
A: 12 languages including English, Hebrew, Arabic, French, German, Spanish, and more — change in Settings.

## Gameplay

**Q: Questions per round?**  
A: 10 multiple-choice questions per round.

**Q: Hard Mode?**  
A: Home-screen toggle limits every question to 12 seconds for maximum challenge and special achievements.

**Q: Offline?**  
A: Yes — questions are stored in the app. Leaderboards, ads, and purchases need internet.
