## What is Time Since?

Streak tracker for iOS and Android (**under construction**). Quit & build modes, goal rings, widgets, share cards.

## Getting started

**Q: Do I need an account?**  
A: No. Open the app and tap Add a streak.

**Q: How do I add my first streak?**  
A: Name it, choose quit or build mode, optionally set a goal (7, 30, 90 days), and save.

**Q: What if I slip or miss a day?**  
A: Reset in one tap. Your all-time best record is preserved in Records.

## Modes

**Quit / avoiding:** Count time since you stepped away — smoking, drinking, social media, etc.  
**Build:** Stack consecutive days for exercise, reading, meditation, or daily practice.

## Widgets & share

**Q: How do I add a home screen widget?**  
A: Long-press home screen → Widgets → Time Since → pick streak and style. Premium unlocks up to 5 widgets and ring style.

**Q: Can I share a milestone?**  
A: Yes — generate a streak card image from the app and post to social or messages.

## Premium

**Free:** Up to 3 streaks, 1 widget, ad-supported.  
**Premium (one-time):** Unlimited streaks, 5 widgets, extra styles, no ads. Product ID: com.zuki.apps.timesince.premium. Restore from Settings.

## Data & privacy

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: On your device only. Uninstalling removes streaks. No cloud account for streak content.

**Q: Is this a medical or sobriety treatment app?**  
A: No — personal tracking tool only. Consult a professional for substance use support.
