# FAQ — Zuli Collage — Photo Collage Maker

Canonical page: https://zukiapps.com/zuli-collage

## What is Zuli Collage?

Zuli Collage is a photo collage maker for iOS (Android coming soon). Pick 2–10 photos, choose a layout, style your collage, and save or share — usually in under 30 seconds.

## Where can I download Zuli Collage?

Free on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/il/app/zuli-collage/id6774583525) for iPhone and iPad. Google Play for Android is coming soon.

## Do I need an account?

No. Zuli Collage works entirely on your device. There is no sign-up, login, or cloud storage of your photos.

## How many photos can I use?

Between 2 and 10 photos per collage. Layouts automatically match your photo count.

## Which languages are supported?

English and Hebrew, including full right-to-left (RTL) layout for Hebrew.

## Is Zuli Collage free?

Yes, the full editor is free. Optional Zuli Collage Premium (one-time purchase) removes ads and the export watermark, enables higher-resolution export, transparent PNG export, and batch save from history.

## How do I reorder photos?

On the picker screen, long-press and drag thumbnails. Numbers on the layout preview match photo order (1 = first photo).

## Why don't some layouts appear?

Layouts are filtered by how many photos you selected. Pick more or fewer photos to see different options.

## Can I zoom or move a single photo inside its cell?

Yes. In the editor, tap a photo to select it, then pinch to zoom, drag to pan, and use two-finger rotate where supported.

## What are Zuli Monsters?

48 original sticker characters created by Zuki Apps. Use them inside collages or install packs for WhatsApp and iMessage.

## How do I add stickers to WhatsApp?

Home → Zuli Monsters → Add to WhatsApp → confirm in WhatsApp.

## What is Layout Studio?

A tool to design custom collage layouts: numbered slots you drag, resize, and shape, then save for reuse.

## Where are collages saved?

Exported collages go to your device photo gallery (with your permission). Zuli Collage also keeps a local My Collages history (up to 30 items).

## Why is there a watermark?

The free tier adds a small Zuli Collage watermark. Premium removes it.

## Are my photos uploaded to your servers?

No. Photos stay on your device unless you explicitly save or share them.

## What data does Zuli Collage collect?

Anonymous crash reports and analytics (Firebase Crashlytics + Analytics) to improve stability. See [Privacy Policy](https://zukiapps.com/zuli-collage/privacy).

## Premium didn't activate after purchase

Tap Restore purchases in Settings. Use the same Apple ID or Google account as your purchase.
