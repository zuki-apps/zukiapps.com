# FAQ — Track Ledger

Canonical page: https://zukiapps.com/track-ledger

## What is Track Ledger?

A simple GPS / GNSS track logger focused on data export and field use. It records your path as raw points with optional time- or distance-based sampling.

## Is it a fitness or social app?

No. There are no leaderboards, workouts, or social feeds. It's built for people who need reliable location logs and file exports.

## Do I need an account?

No. Everything is stored locally on your device.

## Does it work offline?

Yes. Recording and viewing saved tracks work without internet. The Map tab needs network to load OpenStreetMap tiles.

## When does the app use my location?

Only while you have an active recording (and optionally in the background if you have Premium and background recording enabled).

## Why did recording pause when I switched apps?

On the free plan, new GPS points are logged only while Track Ledger is in the foreground. Premium with background recording enabled continues logging when you leave the app.

## What does "raw GNSS, no smoothing" mean?

Points are saved as the device reports them. We don't apply a smoothing filter — you get the honest trace for your own analysis.

## How many tracks can I save?

Free: up to 10 completed tracks. Premium: unlimited (or a higher cap you choose in Settings).

## What happens when the ledger is full?

In Settings → Ledger you choose: automatically remove oldest tracks, or refuse new recordings until you delete tracks manually.

## Which formats are free?

CSV export and share are always available.

## Which formats need Premium?

GPX and GeoJSON.

## Can I share a track to another app?

Yes — use Share from track summary or Ledger. The system share sheet opens (Mail, Files, AirDrop, etc.).
