# FAQ — Paratrooper Blitz

Canonical page: https://zukiapps.com/paratrooper-blitz

## Is Paratrooper Blitz free?

Yes. The game is free to download and play. Optional one-time Remove Ads purchase removes banner and interstitial ads.

## What age is the game for?

Rated Teen (13+). Mild military-style arcade action—no gore, no chat, no user-generated content.

## Does it work offline?

Core gameplay works offline. Leaderboards, achievements, ads, and purchases need network and store services.

## Which languages are supported?

English, Arabic, German, Spanish, French, Hebrew, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, and Chinese (Simplified). Change in Settings.

## What’s the difference between Arcade and Classic?

Arcade: slide left/right, shoot up. Classic: rotate turret 360° and aim manually.

## How do I lose?

Four parachutists land on the **same side** of your turret, or a bomb hits the ground.

## Why did my score go down when I fired?

Each shot costs 1 point (classic Paratrooper rule).

## Do paratroopers ever land off-screen?

No—drops stay within the play area.

## Why didn’t my score appear on the leaderboard?

You must sign in to Google Play Games (Android) or Game Center (iOS). Open **Global Rankings** from the home menu once to complete sign-in, then play a full round.

## Are Arcade and Classic scores separate?

Yes—each mode has its own leaderboard.

## How many achievements are there?

14 trophies with an in-app catalog showing requirements.

## What does Remove Ads include?

Removes the home banner and post-game interstitial. One-time non-consumable purchase.
