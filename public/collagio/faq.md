# Collagio — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Collagio?
Collagio is a photo collage maker for iOS and Android. Pick 2–10 photos, choose a layout, style your collage, and save or share—in under 30 seconds.

### Do I need an account?
No. Collagio works entirely on your device. There is no sign-up, login, or cloud storage of your photos.

### How many photos can I use?
Between **2 and 10** photos per collage. Layouts automatically match your photo count.

### Which languages are supported?
English and Hebrew, including full right-to-left (RTL) layout for Hebrew.

### Is Collagio free?
Yes, the full editor is free. Optional **Collagio Premium** (one-time purchase) removes ads and the export watermark, enables higher-resolution export, transparent PNG export, and batch save from history.

## Creating collages

### How do I reorder photos?
On the picker screen, **long-press and drag** thumbnails. Numbers on the layout preview match photo order (1 = first photo).

### Why don’t some layouts appear?
Layouts are filtered by how many photos you selected. Pick more or fewer photos to see different options.

### Can I zoom or move a single photo inside its cell?
Yes. In the editor, **tap a photo** to select it, then **pinch to zoom**, **drag to pan**, and use two-finger rotate where supported.

### How do I add text?
Open the editor → **Text** tab → type in the sheet. Drag text on the canvas to position it; tap to edit again.

### Hebrew text looks wrong in the text field?
Collagio detects script direction automatically. Type normally—the keyboard and cursor follow Hebrew RTL when needed.

### What are canvas shapes?
Canvas shape sets the overall aspect ratio: square, portrait, story (9:16), landscape, circle, rounded square, heart, and classic 4:3.

### How do I add stickers to WhatsApp?
Home → **Zuli Monsters** → **Add to WhatsApp** → confirm in WhatsApp.

### How do I use stickers in iMessage?
Open Messages → tap the **App Store icon** next to the text field → select **Zuli Monsters** → pick a sticker.

### Can I export a transparent sticker?
Yes. On the export screen, choose **Sticker** export (Premium for watermark-free transparent PNG).

## Layout Studio

### What is Layout Studio?
A tool to **design custom collage layouts**: numbered slots you drag, resize, and shape, then save for reuse.

### Can I change photo count after starting?
Yes. Open **More** in the action bar to add or remove slots (within 2–10).

### Where do saved layouts appear?
In the layout picker when creating a new collage, under your saved layouts.

## Saving & sharing

### Where are collages saved?
Exported collages go to your **device photo gallery** (with your permission). Collagio also keeps a local **My Collages** history (up to 30 items).

### Why is there a watermark?
The free tier adds a small Collagio watermark. Premium removes it.

### Can I share directly to Instagram or WhatsApp?
Yes. Use **Share** on the export screen to open the system share sheet.

## Privacy & data

### Are my photos uploaded to your servers?
No. Photos stay on your device unless you explicitly save or share them.

### What data does Collagio collect?
Anonymous crash reports and analytics (Firebase Crashlytics + Analytics) to improve stability. See our [Privacy Policy](https://zukiapps.com/collagio/privacy).

## Troubleshooting

### A photo shows as a dark box
The original file may have been deleted from your gallery. Re-pick the photo from your library.

### Export failed
Check storage permission and free space. Try closing other apps and exporting again.

### Premium didn’t activate
Tap **Restore purchases** in Settings. Use the same Apple ID / Google account as your purchase.

### I lost my work
Collagio can offer to **resume a draft** when you reopen the app if you left mid-edit.
