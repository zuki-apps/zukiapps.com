# FAQ — Sudoku Fun Go - Classic Sudoku

Canonical page: https://zukiapps.com/sudoku-puzzle

## Do I need an account?

No. Open the app and tap New Game. Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android) is optional for leaderboards and achievements.

## Is the app free?

Yes. Sudoku Fun Go is free to download and play. Ads support development. You can remove ads with a one-time in-app purchase.

## Which devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad (iOS), and Android phones and tablets with Google Play services. Requires a recent OS version — see the store listing for details.

## What are the difficulty levels?

| Level | For whom | |-------|----------| | Easy | Beginners — fewer empty cells | | Medium | Balanced daily challenge | | Hard | Experienced solvers | | Expert | Maximum empty cells — bravest players only |

## Can I pause mid-game?

Yes. Tap pause; the timer stops until you resume. Your puzzle is saved automatically.

## What happens if I quit?

If you leave without finishing, that puzzle’s progress is lost. Use Continue Game on the home screen to return to an in-progress puzzle.

## How do notes work?

Turn on Notes, tap a cell, then tap digits to mark candidates. When you place a confirmed number, matching notes in the row, column, and box can auto-clear (if enabled in Settings).

## Do hints hurt my score?

Yes. Each hint costs 300 points from your final score. Use them when stuck, but fewer hints = higher rank.

## How many mistakes are allowed?

Mistakes are tracked and reduce your score (−500 each). The puzzle does not end on a wrong entry — you can keep solving.

## How is my score calculated?

Base = 10,000 × difficulty multiplier (Easy ×1, Medium ×2, Hard ×3, Expert ×5) Minus 2 points per second, 500 per mistake, 300 per hint. Example: Expert, 10 minutes, 0 mistakes, 0 hints → up to 48,800 before time penalty details.

## Why is my leaderboard empty?

Sign in to Game Center or Play Games, complete a puzzle to submit a score, then open Statistics → Friends or Global. Install from the Play Store or App Store (not sideload) for full Play Games / billing support on Android.

## How do achievements unlock?

Automatically when you meet goals (first win, streaks, flawless solve, etc.). Open Statistics → Achievements to view progress.
