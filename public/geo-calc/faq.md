## What is GEO Calc?

Offline WGS84 coordinate tools for field professionals. Live on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/il/app/geo-calc-coordinates/id6787519144); Google Play coming soon. Convert DD, DMS, UTM, and MGRS; Vincenty geodesic distance and bearing; map measure; waypoints and GPX export.

## Where can I download GEO Calc?

Free on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/il/app/geo-calc-coordinates/id6787519144) for iPhone and iPad. Google Play for Android is coming soon.

## Offline & accuracy

**Q: Does GEO Calc need internet?**  
A: Core conversion, distance, and map measure work offline. Ads and analytics may use network when available.

**Q: Is it survey-grade?**  
A: Uses standard WGS84 geodesy (Vincenty, ellipsoidal area). Not a substitute for certified survey instruments or legal boundaries.

**Q: Do I need GPS?**  
A: Converter and distance tools work from typed coordinates. GPS is optional for map centering.

## Formats & export

**Q: What formats are supported?**  
A: Decimal Degrees (DD), DMS, UTM, and MGRS on WGS84.

**Q: Can I export GPX?**  
A: Yes — save waypoints locally and export GPX 1.1 for GIS or GPS devices.

## Account & pricing

**Q: Do I need an account?**  
A: No. Waypoints stay on your device.

**Q: Is there a subscription?**  
A: No. v1.0 is free with ads. No in-app purchases at launch.
