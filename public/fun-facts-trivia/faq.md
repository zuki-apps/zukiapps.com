# FAQ — Fun Facts! Trivia

Canonical page: https://zukiapps.com/fun-facts-trivia

## What is Fun Facts! Trivia?

A free mobile trivia game by Zuki Apps. Each round has 10 timed multiple-choice questions across eight fact categories, with scoring, streaks, stats, and optional leaderboards.

## Is it free?

Yes. The app is free to download and play. Optional Premium removes ads with a one-time purchase (not a subscription).

## iOS and Android?

Yes — available on the App Store and Google Play.

## Do I need an account?

No account is required to play. Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android) is only needed for leaderboards and achievements.

## How many questions per game?

10 questions per round.

## What categories are available?

Science & Nature · History & Culture · Geography & World · Odd Facts & Records · Movies & Film · Amazing Facts · Sports & Games · World Records.

## What are the difficulty levels?

- Easy — 20 seconds per question - Medium — 15 seconds - Hard — 12 seconds Harder modes increase score multipliers.

## How does scoring work?

Points come from base value × difficulty, plus time bonus, plus streak bonus (up to +50%), plus a +150 perfect-round bonus if you get 10/10. See the [user manual](../how-to/user_manual.md) for details.

## What happens when time runs out?

The question counts as incorrect and your streak resets.

## Are questions the same every time?

Questions are drawn from a large bundled bank (~395 per language) and shuffled so rounds feel fresh.

## Can I play offline?

Yes. Questions are stored in the app. Leaderboards, ads, and purchases need internet.

## Which languages are supported?

Arabic, German, English, Spanish, French, Hebrew, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, and Chinese (with regional variants where available).
