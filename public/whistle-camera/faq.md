## What is Whistle Camera?

Hands-free camera app. Whistle to take a photo or (Pro) start/stop video. Practice mode calibrates detection to your whistle.

## Detection troubleshooting

Lower sensitivity (level 1), widen frequency band, enable debug overlay, use Practice whistle in Settings.

## Internet required?

Core capture works offline. Ads, analytics, and purchases may need network.

## Microphone

On-device whistle detection only — not stored as audio recordings.

## Storage

In-app gallery; optional copy to Photos album “Whistle Camera”.

## Pro

Whistle video start/stop, premium modes, no ads. Restore purchases in Settings.
