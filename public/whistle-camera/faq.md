## What is Whistle Camera?

Hands-free camera app for iOS and Android. Whistle to take a photo or (with Pro) start and stop video. Practice mode calibrates detection to your whistle. Works offline for core capture.

## How do I take hands-free selfies?

Prop or hold your phone, enable whistle detection, and whistle when you are in frame. The shutter fires instantly — no timer sprint. Use Practice mode in Settings to calibrate first.

## Pet and dog photos

Whistle to get your pet’s attention, then Whistle Camera captures in the same moment. Calibrate with Practice whistle in Settings for reliable detection in quiet or noisy environments.

## Detection troubleshooting

Lower sensitivity (level 1), widen the 0–100 frequency band in Settings, enable the debug overlay, and use Practice whistle to calibrate from session peaks.

## Is Whistle Camera free?

Free download on iOS and Android. Optional Pro (one-time): whistle video start/stop, premium modes, no ads. Restore purchases in Settings.

## Internet required?

Core capture works offline. Ads, analytics, and purchases may need network.

## Microphone

On-device whistle detection only — audio is not stored as recordings.

## Storage

In-app gallery; optional copy to Photos album “Whistle Camera”.

## Timer alternative

Countdown 3, 5, or 10 seconds works with or without whistle detection.
