# FAQ — Power Interval Timer - Tabata & HIIT

Canonical page: https://zukiapps.com/power-interval-timer

## What is Power Interval Timer?

A minimal interval timer for Tabata, HIIT, and custom work/rest rounds. It runs offline and focuses on clarity mid-workout.

## Do I need an account?

No. Timers are stored on your device.

## Does it work without internet?

Yes. Core timing, sounds, and saved timers work offline. Ads and purchase verification may need connectivity when those features are used.

## Which devices are supported?

iPhone, iPad, Android phones/tablets, Apple TV, and Android TV.

## Can I do classic Tabata?

Yes — 20 s work, 10 s rest, 8 rounds (adjust prepare/cooldown as you like).

## Can I skip rest or end early?

Use **Skip** for the current phase or **Stop** to end the session.

## How do I pause with gloves on?

Tap anywhere on the timer face to start/pause.

## How many clock styles are there?

Ten display modes. Three are free (Circular, Digital, Linear); seven are Premium.

## Can I use my own photo on a timer?

Yes — pick from your gallery when creating/editing a timer (system photo picker on modern Android).

## Dark mode?

Settings → Theme: Light, Dark, or System.

## What’s included in Premium?

Unlimited saved timers, all display modes, no ads. One-time purchase (not a subscription).

## How much is Premium?

Price is shown in the app from your local App Store or Google Play (varies by country).
